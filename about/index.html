A little bit about me.

I started taking classes with Skillcrush when I realized that, as an active duty military spouse, the idea of having a dedicated career in one single city (or state, or country for that matter) was not feasible. Working as a content editor for a collector car auction company in Arizona, I decided that while I won't be able to take my job with me on our next move, I could be doing SO much more than just editing — and without the restrictions

of being in a dedicated office setting. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I'd found something amazing that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work, and something that challenged me to develop more and more new skills. Now I'm dedicated to making the full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to do what I love.
